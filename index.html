Superhero Ascension Protocol

A Field Guide Based on the Heroic Journey of Captain Cluck

Document ID: CC-SP1

Prepared by: The International Institute of Extraordinary Poultry

Confidentiality Level: 🥚 Top Eggshell Secret

🐣 Section 1: Introduction

Becoming a superhero is no mere flap of the wings. It is an honour, a duty, and—most importantly—a transformation. The Superhero Ascension Protocol is designed to identify, prepare, and empower extraordinary individuals (avian or otherwise) for lives of justice, courage, and dramatic rooftop posing.

Modelled on the meteoric rise of Captain Cluck—from humble coop to skyborne legend—this field guide lays out the crucial stages of heroic evolution, from hatching to heroism. Whether you're a feathered fledgling or a bipedal being with aspirations of greatness, your journey begins here.

🧬 Section 2: Eligibility Requirements

To qualify for the Superhero Ascension Protocol, you must meet the following criteria:

  • 🌀 Born during a weather anomaly, such as a thunderstorm, double rainbow, solar eclipse, or aggressive fog.
  • 🧭 Possess moral cluckpulsion—a deep inner urge to protect others, regardless of flock or species.
  • 💥 Survived a near-egg-sperience, such as a brush with mortality, existential poultry panic, or predator ambush.
  • 🦊 Show resistance to foxes, propaganda, or bureaucratic nonsense.
  • 💡 Exhibit spontaneous bursts of insight during molting seasons.
  • 🎶 Unusual talent, such as rhythmic clucking, skywriting, or telepathic communication with barn cats

Note: Traits such as excessive pride, greed for mealworms, or chronic coop gossip disqualify a candidate immediately.

⚡ Section 3: The Empowerment Event (a.k.a. “The Peckoning”)

Empowerment is unpredictable, cosmic, and often unrepeatable. It marks the moment the candidate’s heroic potential is fully activated, usually via exposure to a rare anomaly:

  • 🍿 Gamma-irradiated corn kernel ingestion (may cause glowing talons).
  • ⛈️ Freak storm interaction with coop wiring or enchanted roosts.
  • 💊 Ingestion of prototype yolk-based isotopes from rogue government programs.
  • 📦 Accidental contact with ancient relics, such as the Golden Egg of Yolk’dmir or the Cluckatrix Crown.

Captain Cluck’s Peckoning occurred when she shielded hatchlings during a coop-collapse storm, and was struck by an arc of unknown origin—believed to be part solar flare, part maternal instinct.

🧪 Section 4: Powers Manifestation Checklist

Upon transformation, candidates may exhibit one or more powers. These should be documented and tested in a controlled free-range environment under the supervision of a licensed Coopvisor.

Powers Description
🦴 Ultra Scratch Strength Can decimate dirt, concrete, and intruder shins. Excellent for rescue ops and intimidation.
🪶 Feather Shield Generates protective gusts with a single wingbeat. Stops arrows, words, and bureaucratic forms.
🐤 Peck of Justice High-speed, beak-first impact. Warning: Do not use on mirrors.
💣 Eggsplosive Orbs Laid only in high-stress conditions. Handle with caution. Can trigger minor shockwaves and major omelettes.
🧠 Unbreakable Courage Immune to fear, intimidation, and marketing schemes. Powers extend to allies nearby.
🔊 Battle Cluck Resonant vocalisation capable of stunning enemies and motivating allies across a 3-acre radius.

Candidates may develop unique, coop-specific powers (e.g. Seed Manipulation, Dustbath Time Distortion).

🧵 Section 5: Costume & Branding

Hero identity is nothing without style. A carefully designed costume inspires allies, unnerves villains, and looks phenomenal on trading cards.

Required Elements:

  • Emblem: Personalised crest or initials (e.g., “CC”). Should appear on chest, shoulder, or eggshell.
  • Cape: Optional, but highly encouraged. Aids in gliding, intimidation, and photo ops. Flame- and peck-resistant.
  • Utility Belt: Must include: mini cluck-kit, commemorative medallion, feed pellets, smoke bombs (chaff-style), and a backup communicator.
  • Boots/Claw Wraps: For grip, shock absorption, and dramatic stances.

Recommended Colours:

  • 🔴 Crimson (for courage)
  • 💛 Yellow (for hope)
  • ⚫ Black (for stealth missions and formality)

Avoid beige, olive, or coop-camo unless you're undercover.

📜 Section 6: The Hero’s Coopmandments

All registered heroes shall abide by the following:

  1. 🛡️ Defend the innocent — feathered or not.
  2. 💢 No villain shall go un-pecked.
  3. 🌟 Always preen with honour.
  4. 🚸 Never cross the road without a clear mission and safety check.
  5. 🪺 Leave every nest better than you found it.
  6. 🗣️ Speak for those who have no voice (especially ducklings).
  7. 🐔 Honour those who came before — especially Captain Cluck.

Infractions may result in temporary de-caping or mandatory public nest-cleaning service.

🧓 Section 7: Retirement & Legacy Planning

No hero flaps forever. When the time comes to step aside, proper legacy planning ensures the flame is passed, not extinguished.

Retirement Conditions:

  • ✅ Defeating an arch-nemesis or saving a full flock.
  • 📖 Completing at least three major heroic arcs.
  • 🎓 Successfully training a certified Sidekick (see Hatchery Program Doc 14-EGG).

Ceremony: A golden yolk-dipping of the feathers, 21-cluck salute, and an honorary egg-shaped plaque in the Hall of Wings.

Captain Cluck’s mysterious disappearance over the Winds of Destiny has yet to be resolved. Her signal beacon still glows—faintly—in the upper skies.

📎Appendix A: Common Missteps to Avoid

  • ❌ Using Eggsplosive Orbs in the roost.
  • ❌ Mistaking villain monologues for dialogue opportunities.
  • ❌ Allowing capes near open fires or threshers.
  • ❌ Accepting corn from masked strangers.
  • ❌ Falling for the classic “chicken crossing the road” bait trap.
  • ❌ Trusting foxes. Period.
